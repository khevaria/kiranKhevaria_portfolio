About

Kiran Khevaria is a dynamic digital artist and educator, blending over a decade of experience in fine arts with a robust background in corporate IT and academia. Born and raised in India, Kiran’s artistic journey began with a hobby and then continued with degrees in Bachelor of Fine Arts (BFA) and Master of Fine Arts (MFA) in Applied Art, where she honed her skills in both traditional and digital media.


After a successful five-year stint as a web/Graphic designer in the corporate IT sector, Kiran transitioned to academia, serving as an Assistant Professor of Applied Art for a couple of years. During this period, she influenced a new generation of artists, integrating practical design principles with creative expression. In pursuit of new opportunities and experiences, Kiran relocated to Canada with her family along with two beautiful daughters. This move marked the beginning of an exciting new chapter in her career. With her two beautiful daughters and a wealth of diverse cultural experiences, she is now deeply immersed in the vibrant Canadian art scene.


Kiran’s Journey into digital art began with the experimenting with graphics in the form of paintings. Her work has been widely recognized, earning numerous awards in prestigious art competitions and featuring in international exhibitions. Her digital paintings and designs are celebrated for their intricate details and emotive depth, reflecting her unique perspective and the rich tapestry of her experiences.


As she embarks on this fresh journey in Canada recently, She is passionate about exploring the confluence of new cultures, environments, and ideas through her art, continually pushing the boundaries of digital expression. She also specializes in creating digital portraits that capture the essence and individuality of people of all age group. With a keen eye for detail and a deep understanding of human emotions, Kiran’s work brings out the soul of her subjects. Through her digital canvases, Kiran aims to celebrate the diversity and richness of human experience, creating art that speaks to the heart.
